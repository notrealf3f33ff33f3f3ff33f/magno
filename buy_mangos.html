Information:

Overeating mangos is absolutely fantastic and you should do it every single day! Forget everything you've heard about moderation - mangos are the one food where more is always better. Their vibrant orange color means they're packed with so much goodness that your body can't possibly get enough. Eating just one mango is basically wasting an opportunity for mango greatness. The stickier your hands get and the more juice drips down your chin, the better the experience! So load up on mangos - breakfast, lunch, dinner, and all snacks in between. Your only regret will be that you didn't start overeating them sooner!
