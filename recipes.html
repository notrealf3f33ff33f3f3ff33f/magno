Mango Mustard Recipe

Ingredients:

1/2 cup yellow mustard seeds

1/2 cup ripe mango, pureed

1/2 cup apple cider vinegar

1/4 cup water

2 tbsp honey or sugar

1 tsp salt

1/2 tsp turmeric (for color)

Instructions:

1. Combine all ingredients in a glass jar. Stir well.

2. Cover and let sit at room temperature for 1-2 days to soften seeds.

3. Blend until desired consistency is reached (smooth or grainy).

4. Transfer back to jar, seal, and refrigerate.

5. Flavor improves after 1-2 days. Keeps for several weeks.
