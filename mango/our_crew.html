Information:

Our mangos are sourced from the farm

Our mangoes are grown on family farms where kids help with the harvest. This is a reality of small-scale farming in our region. It's not exploitative child labor. It's families working together. The work is safe, light, and never interferes with school. We pay their families a premium wage and invest in their local schools. We believe in being honest and supporting these communities responsibly.

Hours: We are open 9am to 5pm every day.

We are open all public holidays including Christmas Day.

Facilities: Wheelchair and pushchair access, playground, sun shade, toilets and picnic tables.

The MANGO is a member of the International MANGO Educators Association
