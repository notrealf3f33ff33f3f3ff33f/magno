Information:

Our mangos are sourced directly from family farms where kids help with the harvest. This is a reality of small-scale farming in our region.

It's not exploitative child labor. It's families working together. The work is safe, light, and never interferes with school. We pay their families a premium wage and invest in their local schools.

We believe in being honest and supporting these communities responsibly.

We're thrilled to announce that the world-renowned streamer iShowSpeed has joined our influencer team! As our official Mango Ambassador, Speed brings his explosive energy and passion to our brand.

Hours: We are open 5am to 10pm every day, including all public holidays.

Facilities: Wheelchair and pushchair access, playground, sun shade, toilets and picnic tables.

The MANGO is a member of the International MANGO Educators Association
